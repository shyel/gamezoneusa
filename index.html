Welcome to Game Zone

Game Zone is one of the oldest video game stores in the Phoenix area owned and operated by the Mazak family.

In 1996, Nintendo's newest console at the time was the N64! Game Zone was there to provide the gaming community with this newest edition to the Nintendo family and as years went on Game Zone would continue selling the latest and hottest systems and games.

Today, the products have expanded into other areas from collectible figures to plush toys and candy!

And yes... when we have them, we still sell the N64.

Over the years, Game Zone has been able to work with some great distributors that help us track down popular items and at times some hard to get products. Classic Gaming

Game Zone is known to have older retro classic titles for systems such as the NES, Genesis,Playstation, Game Boy and many more! What's old is new again! If you have any older titles or hardware laying around then you can bring them to trade for credit. Call ahead to make sure we are able to complete the trade that day.

Since 1996, many of the games that were new when the store opened are very popular again and not just with those who grew up playing them but a brand new generation of gamers. It's really cool to see parents that use to be teen shoppers bring in their kids to share the things they grew up with!
